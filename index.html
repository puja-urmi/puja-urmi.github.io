About

I am currently a Healthcare AI and Data Scientist at mlHealth360, working on AI research and development for Orion, a radiology assistant platform. I hold an M.A.Sc. in Computer Engineering from the University of Guelph (CGPA: 3.93/4.00) and a B.Sc. in Biomedical Engineering from Bangladesh University of Professionals (CGPA: 3.68/4.00).

My research developed Adaptive Differentially Private Federated Learning (ADP-FL) for privacy-preserving medical image segmentation, achieving up to 14% Dice score improvements on benchmarks like KiTS23, BraTS24 and FeTS24 in real federated setup.
