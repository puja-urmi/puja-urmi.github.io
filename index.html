About me

I am a researcher focused on federated learning, medical image segmentation, and deep learning. Currently working on projects related to pose estimation and medical image analysis.

My Projects

  • Multiclass Semantic Segmentation: Developed a segmentation model for kidney and tumor regions in CT images, achieving notable performance on the KiTS19 dataset.
  • Federated Learning for Medical Image Segmentation: Built a federated learning system that dynamically adjusts data transformations and model weights based on image quality for medical image segmentation tasks.
  • RAG-based Medical Chatbot: Designed a medical chatbot using Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) for answering health-related queries.
  • Human Pose Estimation: Worked on a deep learning model for real-time human pose estimation, applicable in healthcare, fitness, and animation.
  • Self-Adaptive Framework for Medical Image Analysis: Created a dynamic image enhancement framework that adapts based on the statistics of the dataset, used for segmentation and classification tasks in medical images.
