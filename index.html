ABOUT

I am an M.A.Sc. candidate in Computer Engineering at the University of Guelph, Canada, holding a Bachelor's degree in Biomedical Engineering. My current research focuses on privacy-preserving federated learning for medical image analysis. In parallel with my research, I have gained valuable experience as a Graduate Teaching Assistant, supporting the delivery of various courses over multiple semesters.

Currently, I am working as a Healthcare AI and Data Scientist at mlHealth 360, where I work on multimodal AI model development for real clinical workflow. This role allows me to apply cutting-edge AI techniques to solve practical healthcare challenges and improve patient outcomes.

Upon completing my Master's in 2025, I am seeking Ph.D. opportunities or roles as an AI/ML researcher to further advance my knowledge and apply my expertise to solving real-world challenges through impactful, AI-driven innovations.
