About

I am a Healthcare AI and Data Scientist at mlHealth360, building advanced deep learning pipelines for Orion and Scaida-radiology AI assistants that streamline diagnostic interpretation and automate clinical report generation using real-world hospital data.

I hold an M.A.Sc. in Computer Engineering from the University of Guelph (CGPA: 3.93/4.00) with a B.Sc. in Biomedical Engineering. I am recipient of multiple scholarships, including the Queen Elizabeth II Graduate Scholarship. My master's research focused on optimizing Differentially Private Federated Learning (ADP-FL) for privacy-preserving medical image segmentation, achieving a 14% Dice score improvement across KiTS23, BraTS24, and FeTS24 benchmarks.

My research interests encompass AI for Healthcare in all its forms, with a focus on developing intelligent, secure, and reliable models that advance clinical diagnostics and decision-making.
